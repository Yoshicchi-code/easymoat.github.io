Concept

Built around your favorite players

Most apps are built around teams. BaseballWatch is built around players. Follow the players you love — across teams, throughout the entire game lifecycle.

Player-Centric View

Your watch screen is organized around your registered players, not teams. The players you care about appear front and center.

Full Game Lifecycle

Before the game (start time), during the game (live BSO and score), and after the game (final result). One screen, the entire day.

Cross-Team Following

Track players from any team in any league. Build a cross-team roster only you would put together.

Custom Roster Building

Up to 20 player slots — enough for a starting lineup plus backups. Build your fantasy roster, your team's entire bench, or a personal all-star team.

Features

Real-time delivery, ready when you are

Server-side polling, push notifications, and a clean live view designed for the small screen on your wrist.

🔔

Real-Time Event Notifications

Hits, home runs, stolen bases, walks, RBIs, strikeouts, runs allowed and more — pushed the moment they happen.

📣

On-Field Appearance Alerts

Get notified when your player takes the field — pitcher start, relief appearance, exit, or batter at-bat. Never miss a moment.

📊

Live Game View

See live score, base-out-strike count, and current pitcher / batter at a glance on your watch.

Season Stats

Up-to-date season totals — AVG, HR, RBI, OPS for batters; W/L, ERA, K, WHIP, SV for pitchers.
